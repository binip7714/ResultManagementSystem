The bachelor of engineering of the Institute of Engineering (IOE), TU being implemented in the College are in Computer, Electronics and Communication, and Civil and Bachelor of architecture, BSc in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT) of Institute of Science and Technology and Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) Faculty of Huamanities and Social Science.
